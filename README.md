

## AlphaForge AI — Multi-Agent Investment Research Platform

## Overview

AlphaForge AI is a multi-agent investment research platform that performs institutional-grade company analysis using specialized AI agents.

Instead of relying on a single AI response, AlphaForge AI coordinates multiple analyst agents—including Business, Financial, Market Intelligence, Risk, and Valuation Analysts—to generate a comprehensive investment recommendation.

The platform is built using Next.js, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, LangGraph, and modern AI engineering practices.
